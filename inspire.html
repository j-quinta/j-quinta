Michael Reynolds

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

Michael Reynolds is the person to me that showed how we should be living. Radical sustainability in architecture is a passion of mine to say the least!

  • Outrageously courageous - He disturbed the status quo and defined a new and innovative way to live.
  • Motivates others - people follow a great leader
  • Big footprint - impact on millions and change the world
  • Integrity - they live true to their calling

Michael Reynolds inpired me to make sustainable living a goal. His architecture shows that our homes shouldnt be confined to a building code, and that they should be more than just a house.
